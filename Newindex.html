The Mohor Promise

In a world of fast fashion, Mohor Clothings stands as a testament to the enduring beauty of handcrafted garments. We specialize in well-designed, premium outfits that empower the modern woman—whether she is presenting at a university viva, leading a meeting in the office, or attending a formal evening event.

Each stitch is a story, and each fabric is chosen for its luxury feel and durability. Our khaadi and handcrafted collections are designed in Bangladesh, for the world.
